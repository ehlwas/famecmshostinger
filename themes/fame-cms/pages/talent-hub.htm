Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide a creative, engaging and safe space for children and young people of determination to learn new skills, develop existing talents and to build confidence.

In the social setting of the Talent Hub, our members have all the benefits of being a part of a unique youth club. Enriching independence whilst encouraging socialising and team ethic as we work towards end of term productions and ultimately the end of year F.A.M.E. event.

Our vision is to be fully inclusive and to ensure integration into the community by enabling the people of determination to fully participate in all spheres of life.
