F.A.M.E. was founded in 2016 by Rosy Ahmed, the CEO of Purple Vogue Events.

F.A.M.E. which represents fashion, art, music & entertainment, is an annual stage show that was launched as a means of encouraging integration and providing a platform for people of determination to showcase their talents.

Using these four channels, F.A.M.E. acts include a fashion show, musical and dance performances and dramatic acts. New participants are encouraged to the stage each year as we develop the show content.

With the growing interest in the annual F.A.M.E. production, we are pleased to be launching the F.A.M.E. TALENT HUB. A place where children and youths from the community can sign up to a variety of recreational classes and enjoy all that the Talent Hub has to offer.
